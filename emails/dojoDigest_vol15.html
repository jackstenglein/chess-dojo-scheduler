Episode 128 | Every World Chess Championship Match Ranked | Part 1: pre-FIDE (1834-1937)

Episode 129 | Every World Chess Championship Match Ranked | Part 2: Soviet Era (1951-1990)
Part 2 focuses on the time after World War II, the Soviet Era of chess between 1951 and 1990, with matches involving Mikhail Botvinnik, Vasily Smyslov, David Bronstein, Mikhail Tal, Tigran Petrosian, Boris Spassky, Bobby Fischer, Anatoly Karpov, Viktor Korchnoi, and Garry Kasparov.

Episode 130 | Every World Chess Championship Match Ranked | Part 3: Modern Era (1993-2023)
