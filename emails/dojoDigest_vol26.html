Updates

The Dojo has been rocked by the news of Danya’s passing. The senseis all knew him personally and almost every Dojoer had enjoyed his online content in some form, whether it was commentary, humor, speedruns, or educational videos. We talked about our memories here and also what we need to do better here.



Revamped Checkmate Puzzles — We've updated our checkmate puzzle interface to be more intuitive and user friendly! Check out the new version here. Soon we'll update the rest of our tactics tests as well.

New Dojo Tournaments — We now have rapid arenas on Friday nights and classical arenas from Saturday to Sunday. Make sure to join our chess.com club to participate in future events.

David was voted Chess.com’s Creator of the Month! Here’s a great interview with him where he talks Dojo and chess.

Need Help Using the Dojo's Site? We now offer weekly Zoom meeting Tuesdays at 1-2pm ET where new members can meet the senseis and ask questions about how to use the site. If you're confused or lost on the site, this is a great opportunity to get your questions answered!

Achievements

Garageheroes has a great new podcast Late 2 Chess where he discusses his journey into chess as an older dude. The man is on a mission!

wolson took 1st place at the Wyoming Championship and Open U1000 on the 22nd and 23rd of October in Casper, Wyoming and gained 139 USCF elo!

Marcupial_misconduct tied for 1st in the U1400 class and tied for 3rd overall at the Lafayette Open Chess Tournament going 3/5! They also beat a 1700 FIDE at that same tournament.

Ryan Cameron just crossed 2000 Chess.com rapid for the first time!

NoseKnowsAll published his Morphy study after six months of work! Part 1 is best for the 600-1000 cohorts, and Part 2 is best for 800-1200. Check it out here.

Dojo Talks Podcast

EP 168 | 2025 Junior Draft Update
The senseis recap last year’s junior draft results and rating swings, then run a snake draft to add three new juniors and two girls each—keeping graduated players for tracking, debating drops and Christopher Yoo’s suspension/return, and highlighting prospects like world junior champion Pranav V, Lu Miaoyi, and even a risky 10-year-old.

EP 169 | Total Chess World Championship Tour
The Dojo crew debates Norway Chess’s new “Total Chess World Championship Tour,” comparing it to past innovations, Magnus Carlsen’s influence, FIDE’s involvement, and the growing clash between classical, freestyle, and online chess circuits.

EP 170 | Remembering Daniel Naroditsky
David, Kostya, and Jesse share their memories of Danya, reflecting on his early talent, kindness, intelligence, humor, and lasting impact on the chess community.

EP 171 | Nootropics
Jesse and Sam dive into how legal, common nootropics might boost chess performance by improving focus, stress-resilience, and brain health, and question where the line lies between smart optimization and unfair advantage.

EP 172 | Justice for Daniel Naroditsky
David, Jesse, and Kostya discuss their thoughts on Daniel Naroditsky and how FIDE should act towards Vladimir Kramnik.

How To Make 1600 elo

GM Jesse Kraai explains the skills and mindset needed to break 1600 on Chess.com, highlighting the importance of positional understanding, long classical games, and learning from common struggles players face at this level.
