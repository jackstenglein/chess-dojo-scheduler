Game and Profile Review

The method of the Game & Profile Review class is simple. You will first meet with your group without the sensei. In this peer review session, you will review a game (or multiple games if there is time) from the user who is next up in the review queue. Try to improve the existing annotations of the game and develop some questions about what was going on in the game.

A few days later, you will meet with the sensei, who will look at the same user. Each sensei session will begin by examining the user's profile and training habits. After that, the group will look at the user's game. The sensei will share their perspective on the game and answer the questions the students came up with during the peer review session. After the sensei session, the user moves to the bottom of the review queue and the process repeats for the next week with the next user in the queue.

Joining Classes

All classes will be conducted through Google Meet. Links to the meetings will be available in the event details on the Dojo calendar. You don't need to download any software ahead of time — simply click the link when it's time for the class to start.

The Queue

You can find your spot in the queue on your ChessDojo profile. If you are not ready to cover your profile, you can mark yourself as paused until you're ready, or leave yourself paused indefinitely. It's fine to participate just by reviewing games from others. 

Communicating with Peers

You can communicate with the other members of your class in a private Discord channel available only to your class. Use this channel to arrange training games, ask organizational questions, get advice, or set up additional game review sessions.

Group Lectures
