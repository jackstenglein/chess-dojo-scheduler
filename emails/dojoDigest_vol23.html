Updates

MyGames Folder - Everyone now has a folder called MyGames – and you can’t get rid of it. The notion is simple: analyzing your games is a pillar of the dojo so everyone should have a folder called MyGames. By default anything you had in your file system will now show up in MyGames and that is a minor hassle for the power users out there – they will have to separate out stuff like their opening files. We did it this way because most Dojoers have used the files system for their own games and we wanted to make things as easy as possible for the majority. So if you simply have your games uploaded you will now find them in the MyGames folder. Our long term ambition with this folder is that we will soon be able to use it for tools like your performance stats and your time management rating.
