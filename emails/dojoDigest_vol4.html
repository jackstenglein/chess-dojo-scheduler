Training Program Updates

Dojo Year Review - View a personalized summary of your journey with the Dojo in 2023! See your rating change, graduations, dojo points and time spent throughout the year. You'll also see how you stack up against your cohort and the Dojo as a whole.

Coaching & Courses - We are now offering live coaching sessions! GM Kraai will begin with a five-week game analysis class, starting tomorrow, Jan 2nd. Join here. Participation is limited to 5 members in the 1000-1400 cohorts, but stay tuned for future classes! For those wanting something to go at their own pace, our opening courses are now available for free tier members, without needing a training plan subscription!

Model Annotated Games - The model annotated games from each cohort have been updated. The Dojo continues to improve the art of game analysis, and we want to showcase the best of the best. These games will be used as the basis for our upcoming book How to Analyze Your Games.

Scorebook - We've released the ChessDojo Scorebook! It's a great way to keep all your OTB games in one place for later review. For those just starting out OTB, view all our tournament gear recommendations here.

Game Explorer - We've added an opening explorer to the site! This allows Dojoers to search for a position in both the Dojo database and the Lichess database. You can also subscribe to a position to get notified when a new game is added to the Dojo database containing that position.

Achievements

Chris Coski continues his march past 1800 USCF and shows that old dudes can still make progress! Check out his excellent blog post here.

Pifflesticks has quit his job and committed himself fulltime to chess for the next year! Listen to his story on the Perpetual Chess podcast and follow along with his journey on his YouTube channel. He's already had lessons with Andras Toth and Eric Rosen and played his first OTB tournament!

Benwick finished 3rd out of 23 players in his club's annual rapid tournament. The top 7 finishers were rated between 1900 and 2450, except Benwick at 1487. Benwick credits Polgar Mates for the strong performance, due to finding M3 while down material in the final round.

Similarly, Jaimeby finished 3rd out of 30 players in his club's 45+30 tournament with a score of 5/7. You can find two of his annotated games from the tournament here and here.

Kostya and David played some nice games in Spain at the Sunway Sitges tournament, both finishing with a score of 6/10. Kostya has annotated his first-round game and uploaded it to the Dojo database here. Kostya has also continued on to Sweden, where he is currently playing in the Rilton Cup, with a score of 3/5 with 4 rounds left.

Admonishment

Too many Dojoers are in the wrong cohort! This is not only bad for the player, but also for the cohort. When you reach the rating for the next cohort, graduate! You don't need to complete all the tasks in your current cohort first. And if you drop down below your current cohort, switch cohorts. We will soon have a popup that pesters those who need to promote or demote themselves.
